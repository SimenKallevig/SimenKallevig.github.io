Privacy Policy

Headlinr is built to support discovery, community and concert culture while treating user data with care.

Headlinr stores account information such as name, email address, profile picture and activity created within the app in order to provide the service, show profiles, and synchronize your experience across devices.

User content

Content you choose to publish may be visible to other users of the service.

We do not sell personal data or share unnecessary personal information publicly.

Technical providers such as Firebase, Postgres and other infrastructure services may be used for authentication, storage, analytics and operation of the platform.

Users may request account deletion or data removal through the app or by contacting the service operator.
